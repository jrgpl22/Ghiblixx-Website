  • The Cat Returns takes a quiet schoolgirl into a whimsical kingdom of cats.

CHIHIRO OGINO (SPIRITED AWAY)

The brave young girl who learns courage and kindness while trapped in a magical world of spirits. Her growth from scared to strong inspires millions.

Fun fact: Chihiro's journey is often seen as a metaphor for growing up and finding yourself.

KIKI (KIKI'S DELIVERY SERVICE)

A young witch starting her journey of independence. Kiki's courage, kindness, and creativity inspire anyone facing the challenges of growing up.

Fun fact: Kiki's broom-riding scenes are some of the most iconic in animation.

PONYO (PONYO)

A cheerful little fish-girl who wants to become human. Ponyo's innocence and joy remind us of the magic of childhood and the power of love.

Fun fact: Ponyo's vibrant colors and bubbly personality make her one of Ghibli's most visually joyful characters.

TOTORO (MY NEIGHBOR TOTORO)

The lovable forest spirit with a big smile and bigger heart. Totoro has become a symbol of innocence, magic, and the wonder of childhood.

Fun fact:
Totoro was so beloved, he became Studio Ghibli's mascot!

HOWL
(HOWL'S MOVING CASTLE)

A mysterious, charismatic wizard with a big heart and a gentle soul. Howl's story is about love, transformation, and finding peace.

Fun fact:
Howl's magical castle is one of the most imaginative settings in animation history.

SAN (PRINCESS MONONOKE)

A fierce warrior raised by wolves, San fights to protect the forest from destruction. She embodies strength, loyalty, and the conflict between humans and nature.

Fun fact:
San's complex character blurs the line between human and nature, making her one of Ghibli's most powerful heroines.
